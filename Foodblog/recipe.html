Delicious Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

Cook the spaghetti. Fry the bacon. Mix eggs with cheese and combine with spaghetti and bacon. Season with salt and pepper.
